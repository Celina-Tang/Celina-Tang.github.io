Work Experience

Software Developer at Quanser Consultant Inc.

In the summer of 2015, I had a co-op opportunity as a software developer at Quanser Consultant Inc and created 3 demo projects for their new product, Qbot2. My projects centered on demonstrating the functionalities of Qbot 2 and possible experiments that could perform with its control system.

Software Developer at Quanser Inc.

  • "Leader and Follower": Used dead reckoning with XY coordinates from encoders and gyro calculations to track and pursue the leader Qbot; their coordinate locations were updated through the network communication to increase the accuracy of the calculation

FIRST Robotics Team 4001

Team 4001 is a FIRST Robotics team from St. Robert CHS. I, as the team captain, was proud to lead a team of 85 members, dedicated many hours everyday after school to my work in robotics. Walking down the path of discovering my passion, I grew from an oblivious beginner in two years to an experienced programmer, leading the programming subteam. I realized that I had a strong interest in programming, which gave me the motivation to persist and excel in self-learning necessary technical skils. During the heat of competition, I presented our technical report on the robot to the judges, which earned my team the Industrial Design Award. This dedication of working diligently towards a dream eventually allow the team to win the Regional Champion at the Greater Toronto Central Regional Competition and play in World Championship quartfinals at St. Louis, USA.

FIRST Lego League Library Program

The FIRST Lego League Library Program is one of Team 4001's outreach project that aimed to promote STEM within a younger audience from the age 5 to 12. We held one session per week for a period of six weeks, which included lessons on simple programming with Lego Mindstorm, automated robot designs, and etc. Six community FIRST Logo teams were created from this program with many students showing genuine interest and their parents providing constructive support. The success of this program contributed greatly to win the Engineering Inspiration Award at Le Festival de Robotique in Montreal.
